## EP01 已学词汇

### 1. employee
- **音标**：/ɪmˈplɔɪiː/
- **词性释义**：n. 员工
- **语境例句**：We have 100 employees in our company.
- **个人化例句**：I'm an employee at Yinman Technology.
- **搭配**：full-time employee / new employee
- **易混词**：employer（雇主）—— 多了个 -r 就变成老板了

### 2. employer
- **音标**：/ɪmˈplɔɪər/
- **词性释义**：n. 雇主
- **语境例句**：My employer provides free lunch.
- **个人化例句**：Yinman Technology is my employer.
- **搭配**：former employer / current employer
- **易混词**：employee（员工）

### 3. smoothly
- **音标**：/ˈsmuːðli/
- **词性释义**：adv. 顺畅地
- **语境例句**：The meeting went smoothly.
- **个人化例句**：The AI agent system runs smoothly after deployment.
- **搭配**：go smoothly / run smoothly
- **钩子**：smooth（光滑的）+ ly = 像丝滑一样顺畅

### 4. eavesdrop
- **音标**：/ˈiːvzdrɑːp/
- **词性释义**：v. 偷听
- **语境例句**：We'll be listening in — eavesdropping on Anna's interview.
- **个人化例句**：Don't eavesdrop on other people's conversations.
- **搭配**：eavesdrop on sb/sth
- **钩子**：eaves（屋檐）+ drop（滴水）→ 古代在屋檐下偷听

### 5. step into
- **音标**：/step ˈɪntuː/
- **词性释义**：phr.v. 走进
- **语境例句**：Anna stepped into the office for her interview.
- **搭配**：step into a room / step into a new role
- **钩子**：step（踏一步）+ into（进入）= 迈步走进

## EP02 新词汇

### 6. qualifications
- **音标**：/ˌkwɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
- **词性释义**：n. 资质、学历
- **语境例句**：Your qualifications look impressive.（Paul 的原话）
- **个人化例句**：My qualifications include a degree in computer science.
- **搭配**：academic qualifications / professional qualifications
- **钩子**：qualify（合格）+ tion = 让你"合格"的证书/学历

### 7. treasurer
- **音标**：/ˈtreʒərər/
- **词性释义**：n. 财务、出纳
- **语境例句**：I was a treasurer of the debating society.（Anna 的原话）
- **个人化例句**：The treasurer manages the team's budget.
- **搭配**：club treasurer / company treasurer
- **钩子**：treasure（宝藏）+ r = 管钱的人

### 8. budget
- **音标**：/ˈbʌdʒɪt/
- **词性释义**：n. 预算
- **语境例句**：We had a very small budget.（Anna 的原话）
- **个人化例句**：The project budget for the AI agent system was tight.
- **搭配**：manage a budget / within budget / over budget
- **钩子**：程序员常见：cloud budget、project budget

### 9. punctuality
- **音标**：/ˌpʌŋktʃuˈælɪti/
- **词性释义**：n. 守时
- **语境例句**：We like punctuality here.（Paul 的原话）
- **个人化例句**：Punctuality is important in job interviews.
- **搭配**：value punctuality / punctuality record
- **钩子**：punctual（准时的）+ ity → 名词化

### 10. campaign
- **音标**：/kæmˈpeɪn/
- **词性释义**：n. 活动、运动
- **语境例句**：I was involved with a campaign to promote a new range of clothes.（Anna 的原话）
- **个人化例句**：We launched a marketing campaign for our AI product.
- **搭配**：marketing campaign / advertising campaign / election campaign
- **钩子**：camp（营地）→ campaign 最初是"军事战役"，后来泛化为"大型活动"

### 11. arrogant
- **音标**：/ˈærəɡənt/
- **词性释义**：adj. 傲慢的
- **语境例句**：Be confident, not arrogant.（旁白提醒 Anna）
- **个人化例句**：Nobody likes an arrogant colleague.
- **搭配**：sound arrogant / seem arrogant
- **易混词**：confident（自信的）是褒义，arrogant（傲慢的）是贬义
